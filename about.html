About JABEL

Our Mission & Story

JABEL is a survivor-led nonprofit dedicated to empowering those affected by human trafficking, sexual assault, and domestic violence — from the moment of crisis all the way to a life that truly thrives.

Our Mission

Beyond surviving, we focus on thriving

At JABEL — Justice Advocates Battling Exploitation & Lies — our mission is rooted in Anabel's personal journey as a survivor of human trafficking, sexual assault, and domestic violence. As a victim advocate, her work is centered on empowering survivors by providing the tools, guidance, and training necessary to help them rebuild their lives and move forward with strength and dignity.

JABEL takes a survivor-led approach, offering vocational training, survivor scholarships, and personal development programs in collaboration with local businesses. These programs help survivors transition out of exploitative situations, build practical skills, and reclaim their self-worth.

What sets JABEL apart is our focus on the thriving stage — the phase after crisis that few other organizations address. We believe survivors can lead positive, fulfilling lives when given the right support, and we commit to walking alongside them every step of the way.

Our core values are empowerment, accountability, and belief in every survivor's potential. Through collaboration with survivors, nonprofits, and law enforcement, we are dedicated to breaking the cycles of abuse and exploitation.

We are committed to offering survivors the resources they need to foster self-sufficiency and healing — not just to survive, but to truly thrive.

— JABEL Mission Statement

Our Founder

Anabel's Story

Add Anabel's story video here

Anabel Velasquez is the founder of JABEL and a survivor herself. Her experience navigating the aftermath of human trafficking, sexual assault, and domestic violence gave her firsthand insight into what survivors need — and what was missing.

She founded JABEL to fill that gap: to be the organization she wished had existed when she needed help. Today, she leads JABEL with the conviction that every survivor deserves not just safety, but the opportunity to rebuild a life they're proud of.

Anabel's survivor-led philosophy shapes every program, every partnership, and every decision JABEL makes. She brings both personal understanding and professional advocacy expertise to the work every day.
