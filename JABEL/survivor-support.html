If you need help, we're here for you.

You don't have to face this alone. Everything you share with us is confidential, and you'll always speak to a real person — someone who understands and cares.

📞 24/7 Crisis Line: 916-777-2585

If you are in immediate danger, please call 911. Our hotline is for support, guidance, and connecting you with resources.

Additional Resources

Whether you need immediate help or are planning your next step, these resources are here for you.

Housing Support

National Helplines

Additional Crisis Resources

These national hotlines are available 24/7 if you need additional support.

National DV Hotline

Support for domestic violence survivors, 24/7 confidential.

Call 1-800-799-7233 Text "START"

National Human Trafficking Hotline

Report trafficking or get help, 24/7, confidential.

Call 1-888-373-7888 Text "HELP"

RAINN Sexual Assault Hotline

Confidential support for sexual assault survivors.

Call 1-800-656-4673

988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

Mental health crisis support, 24/7.

Call or Text 988
