Our Mission

Beyond surviving — we focus on thriving

At JABEL, our mission is rooted in Anabel's personal journey as a survivor. We empower survivors of human trafficking, sexual assault, and domestic violence with the tools, guidance, and training they need to rebuild their lives and move forward with strength and dignity.

What sets JABEL apart is our survivor-led philosophy. While many organizations focus on crisis intervention, we walk alongside survivors through every stage — including the often-overlooked thriving stage. We offer vocational training, survivor scholarships, and personal development programs in collaboration with local businesses.
