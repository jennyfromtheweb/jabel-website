Transparency

Your Money, Accounted For

We believe you should know exactly where your donation goes. JABEL operates with full financial transparency.

Is my donation tax-deductible? ▾

Yes. JABEL is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization (EIN: 99-3959461). All donations are fully tax-deductible to the extent permitted by law.

How is my donation used? ▾

Donations fund our crisis hotline, vocational training programs, survivor scholarships, housing referrals, legal support, and community outreach. We hold a Candid Gold Seal of Transparency.

Can I set up a recurring monthly donation? ▾

Yes — select "Monthly" in the donation widget above. Monthly donors provide the most reliable support, helping us plan programs and serve more survivors over time.

Are there other ways to give? ▾

Yes! You can also volunteer your time, become a corporate sponsor, organize a fundraiser, or donate in-kind goods and services. See all ways to get involved →
