Survivor Stories

Share Your Story

Survivor stories are one of the most powerful tools for creating change — they build empathy, break stigma, and let other survivors know they are not alone.

JABEL works with survivors who are ready and willing to share their journey — on your own terms, in your own words, with your privacy fully protected. Sharing is always voluntary.

If you're a survivor who wants to share your story, or a supporter who wants to advocate on behalf of someone, we'd be honored to work with you.
